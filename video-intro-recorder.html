Tips for a Great Video

  • Keep it concise (30-60 seconds)
  • Introduce yourself and your expertise
  • Explain how you help clients
  • Mention your major credentials
  • Use good lighting and minimize background noise

Sample Script

"Hi, I'm [Name], a [Profession] with [X] years of experience in [Specialty]. I help clients [specific benefit you provide]. I've worked with [types of clients or notable achievements]. I'd love to help you with [specific problem you solve]. Book a session to get started!"

Why Video Introductions Matter

Experts with video introductions get 3x more bookings than those without. Your video helps establish trust and connection with potential clients before they even book a session.

Optimize Your Video
