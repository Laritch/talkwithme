About Me

I'm Dr. James Wilson, a passionate AI and Machine Learning expert with over 12 years of experience in the field. After completing my PhD in Computer Science with a specialization in AI, I spent 7 years as a research lead at Google AI, where I worked on cutting-edge projects involving natural language processing and computer vision.

Now, I'm dedicated to helping businesses of all sizes implement practical machine learning solutions that drive real results. Whether you're looking to automate processes, extract insights from data, or build custom AI applications, I can guide you through the entire process from concept to implementation.
