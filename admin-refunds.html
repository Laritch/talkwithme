Refund Policy Management

Current Refund Policy

Version 2.3 - Updated March 15, 2023

Time Frame

Clients may request a refund within 14 days of the service completion date.

Refund Amount

Full refunds (100%) are provided for service quality issues or expert unavailability.

Partial refunds (50-80%) are provided for services partially completed or with minor issues.

Cancellation refunds vary based on notice provided (100% if >24h, 50% if <24h).

Documentation

Clients may be required to provide documentation or evidence supporting their refund request in cases where there is a dispute.

Refund Method

Refunds are processed to the original payment method when possible. Platform credits may be offered as an alternative.

Processing Time

Refund requests are processed within 3 business days of approval.

Funds may take an additional 5-10 business days to appear in the client's account, depending on their payment provider.
