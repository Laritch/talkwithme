Frequently Asked Questions

How secure are my payment details?

We use bank-level encryption and never store your full card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by PCI-compliant service providers with industry-leading security standards.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with an expert session, you can request a refund within 7 days. For subscription plans, you can cancel anytime and receive a prorated refund for unused time.

How do experts receive their payments?

Experts can choose their preferred payout method including direct bank transfers, PayPal, or cryptocurrency. Payments are processed on a bi-weekly schedule, with options for instant payouts for verified experts.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees. All processing fees are clearly displayed for each payment method. Subscription plans include all features listed with no additional charges. International payments may include currency conversion fees from your payment provider.

Can I change my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
